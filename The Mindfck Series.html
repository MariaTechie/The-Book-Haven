The Mindf*ck Series

The Mindf*ck series is my absolute favorite! It's a must-read for thriller and crime fans. I went through so many emotions – crying, laughing, anger, and sadness. It was perfect! I wish I could experience it for the first time again. Just a heads-up, check the trigger warnings before reading. It's about Lana Myers, (yeah the author loved Michael Myers) who suffered terrible abuse from multiple men who also murdered her brother. The townspeople didn't help, believing her innocent father was a serial killer. They killed him too. Lana, also known as Victoria, barely survived and wanted revenge. She changed her name and trained for years with her friend (who also wanted revenge). Lana started her mission to kill and torture the men responsible. Then she met FBI agent Logan Bennett, the detective on her case – which is exactly why you should read it! It's about female rage and empowerment, a masterpiece. Sadly, the author passed away in 2019. May she rest in peace.
