Pestilence

Although I liked "Pestilence", I didn't think it was a flawless read. One of the Horsemen of the Apocalypse, Pestilence, captures Sara, a fierce firefighter, as she attempts to stop him. They fall in love while traveling together, which seemed a little hurried considering the situation—he is actually spreading a deadly plague. Although I enjoyed the characters and the grim, post-apocalyptic setting, the romance occasionally seemed a little flimsy, and the plot got a little monotonous. In spite of these problems, I'm looking forward to the series' next novel.
